HOW TO PLAY

Main Menu
  • first you should look at the provided image and remember Its details for one minute.
  • After the time is out, there are a couple of yes/no questions that you should answer.
  • In this game, there is no loss, it just measures how much good your visual memory is.
  • the questions will ask you if a specific hidden object was in the image or not.
  • For every correct choice your score will increase by 5 points.
  • If you choose wrong choice the score will NOT increase.
  • After you complete the level, you can reach the next level.
